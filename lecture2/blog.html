AI

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the capability of computational systems to perform tasks typically associated with human intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and solve problems. It powers technologies like voice assistants, recommendation systems, and self-driving cars. AI can analyze vast amounts of data quickly, making it useful in fields like healthcare, finance, and education. As it continues to evolve, AI is transforming the way we live and work.

Sports

Sports play a vital role in physical fitness, entertainment, and bringing people together globally

Sports are a major part of human culture, promoting physical health, teamwork, and national pride. From cricket and football to athletics and tennis, sports provide both entertainment and inspiration. In India, cricket holds a special place, while other sports like badminton and wrestling are also gaining popularity. Events like the Olympics and World Cup unite fans across the world.
